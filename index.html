I am currently a 4th-year PhD student in Universitat Pompeu Fabra. Before starting my PhD, I earned a B.A. in Hispanic Phinology from Xi'an International Studies University (Xi'an, Shaanxi, China). Then, I pursued an M.A. in Theoretical and Applied Linguistics at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona, Catalunya, Spain), with a specialization in Formal Linguistics and Computational Linguistics.

My PhD thesis aims at exploring the benefits of incorporating pedagogical hand gestures into phonetic training on the learning of L2 sounds. Additionally, my research interests extend to the acquisition of L2 Spanish pronunciation by Mandarin-speaking learners.
